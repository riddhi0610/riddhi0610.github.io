CSC senior director of events

I am a part of University of Waterloo's Computer Science Club Event Coordinator team where I am the senior director of events. Events controls the logistics and events for CSC! We provide CS focused opportunities, socials, competitions, and workshops for UW's students. I have listed events I have planned from August 2021 to currently.
